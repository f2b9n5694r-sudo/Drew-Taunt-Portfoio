Who I am

Notre Dame junior pursuing medicine through service and science.

I’m Drew Taunt, a Neuroscience and Behavior major at the University of Notre Dame with a minor in Compassionate Care in Medicine. My interests connect the biological complexity of the brain with the human side of healthcare.

I’m especially drawn to patient-centered medicine, clinical learning, research, and leadership. My goal is to keep building experiences that make me a more thoughtful student, teammate, leader, and future physician.
